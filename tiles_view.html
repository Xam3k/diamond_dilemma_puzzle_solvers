Diamond Dilemma, all 160 tiles (digitised)

These are the 160 tiles exactly as encoded in this repository, drawn in the same order and arrangement as Jaap Scherphuis's original tile sheets: 8 per row, grouped silver (1 to 32), red (33 to 80), blue (81 to 160). Put this next to Jaap's sheets and compare tile by tile to confirm the digitisation is faithful. Each tile is an up-triangle with edge 0 along the bottom (read right to left), edge 1 up the left side, edge 2 down the right side, which is the convention the data was read with.

Gold lines (the gold challenge)

Source data: diamonddilemma.txt (Jaap's own values) to tiles.json, with within-tile wiring in arcs.json.

White lines (the silver / red / blue challenges)

Source data: whites.txt, wiring in white_arcs.json. Drawn white with a grey halo for visibility.

Rendered by render_tiles_sheet.py. Some of the code and documentation in this project was produced with the assistance of Claude AI models (Anthropic), under human direction and review.
